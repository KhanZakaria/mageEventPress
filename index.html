Event Description

An initiative of BRS Infraventures and Projects Private Limited, our intent behind District Gravity is to create a common playground for those who relish the euphoria of thrill. A place where all inhibitions are shed and you give in to the moment of adventure. We have put together activities for you that are unique in scale and unparalleled in many aspects of the country.
The park is ideal for a great bonding time with friends and family. Tailored modules for team activities make it an ideal corporate outing destination. Various other provisions like Brand Engagement Solutions, Summer Camps, Boot Camps, Live Events, Sports Clinics and more make District Gravity a mecca for anything adventure.

Event Experiences:
Bungee Jump: India’s tallest fixed crane Bungee Jump from 200 feet.
Karting: A 1.2km Karting track which is the first in India commissioned by CIK.
Multi-Activity Tower: 60 feet high Multi-Activity Tower with 6 activities including Wall Climbing, Slithering, Para Jump, Net Climbing, Multiple Climbing, and rappelling.
Rope Course: 50 feet high with 3 levels and various obstacles.
Giant Swing: 50 feet high drop.
Human Slingshot: 120 feet propulsion.
Inflatable Park: 5 fun activities including Bull Ride, Bungee Run, Sumo Wrestling, Meltdown and Human Foosball.
Paintball: An advanced Paintball field for small to large groups
Ball Sports: Compact sports arenas for Basketball, Foo tball, Badminton, and Volleyball
